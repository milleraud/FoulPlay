Foul Play began on March 27, 2021. It was partially due to a ridiculous amount of coding stress and trying to find a way to calm down and have more fun. In our experience, very few people have beef with birds. Most individuals like them, if not love them. With that in mind, we decided to create a company that only focuses on bird games. This is not a big site. People don't like big birds as much. Some people are terrified by ostriches. This is not an ostrich site. We're more of a finch company.
